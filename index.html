Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Fusion Coaching

What courses does Fusion Coaching offer?
Fusion Coaching offers comprehensive programs in Commerce (Class 11 & 12, Accounts, Economics, Business Studies, CUET Commerce) and Science (Class 11 & 12, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Foundation Batches).
What is the batch size at Fusion Coaching?
We maintain small batch sizes of 20-25 students per class to ensure individual attention and personalized learning. This allows our faculty to focus on each student's progress.
Are there any free demo classes available?
Yes! We offer free demo classes every Sunday. You can experience our teaching methodology and interact with faculty members before enrolling. Limited seats available — register in advance.
Does Fusion Coaching provide study materials?
Absolutely. We provide comprehensive digital notes, practice worksheets, sample papers, and reference materials curated by our expert faculty. All materials are regularly updated to match the latest syllabus.
How can I enroll at Fusion Coaching?
You can visit our center at E2/005 Bharat City, Near Teela Mode, call us at +91 87005 17172, or fill out the enquiry form on this website. Our team will guide you through the simple admission process.
Do you offer CUET preparation for Commerce students?
Yes! We have a dedicated CUET Commerce Preparation program with targeted mock tests, strategy sessions, and expert guidance to help you secure admission in top universities like DU, BHU, and JNU.
What is the fee structure at Fusion Coaching?
Our fee structure is designed to be affordable while maintaining premium quality education. We offer flexible payment options and installment plans. Please contact us for detailed fee information specific to your chosen course.
